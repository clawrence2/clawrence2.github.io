Our Process.

01Research & Goal Definition

We take the time to fully understand your business model and goals. This drives everything that we do. From there we do deep research to understand your competitors and position you in a way that sets you apart. Our findings in this phase will guide us the rest of the way through design and development and result in a powerful, targeted online presence.

02Concept Creation

This phase is where your site starts to take shape! Initial ideas about site architecture and necessary pages will be developed and presented. The fundamental vision of your site will take shape. This is also the step where our copywriters will begin to craft content to position your brand above the rest.

03Design

The design phase is one of the most exciting! This is where all of the hard work of the first three phases really starts to take shape. For the first time you’ll see the overall look and feel of your new digital presence! There are a lot of exciting steps in this process, everything from color scheme development to image sourcing.

04Development

A tremendous amount of work went into getting your website looking absolutely perfect in the design phase now it’s time to code it! Our expert developers will take the original designs and literally code them so that they can be accessed via the web. Our developers will make sure that your website is accessible and looks great from just about any device. This takes skill and deep understanding of responsive development - something we excel at!

05Launch

A tremendous amount of research, design and development work has gone into making your site perfect. It’s launch time! This is the birth (or rebirth) of your digital presence on the internet! Our developers are on standby to ensure the launch goes perfectly and begin testing your site on a multitude of devices to ensure everything is perfect. Your online presence is now out there in force!
